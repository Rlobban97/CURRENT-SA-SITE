SA Senate SkyDrive Archive

Due to space constraits on the University web servers documents from past years are archived on SkyDrive. A group "UAlbanySASenateArchives" has been created and individuals must request access to the Archives. Once approved, members can view and download any and all digital files that have been deposited there. To start the process click here and login with a Windows Live ID (if prompted) - your UAlbanyMail account should work, or any Live account will work. You can then request access and within a day or so you should be granted access. If you have issues or need access sooner please contact the webmaster.
