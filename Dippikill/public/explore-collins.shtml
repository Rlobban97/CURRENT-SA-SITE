Collins Lodge was respectfully named for former University at Albany President Evan Collins. The first log lodge constructed at Dippikill this is truly the definition of Adirondack. Collins offers a cook stove for cooking and a grand central fireplace for heat. We supply five (5) gallons of water to start your stay. The sleeping area is an open second floor area. The addition of three skylights add to the natural light in the lodge.
