Here are some guidelines for making a reservation:

- Check the availability of the lodge or campsite you are interested in by contacting the Student Association at (518) 442-5640
- Send a completed Reservation Request Form along with a Certified Check or Money Order payable Student Association.
- Payment Options: Cash, Certified Check, Money Order, Visa and MasterCard.
- Credit Card Reservations may be taken over the telephone.
- You can book up to three months in advance for large functions that require the entire facility.
- There are no refunds, no exceptions.
