Birches Lodge is only a few minutes walking from the parking lot. Up to eight (8) guests can enjoy this log cabin with fireplace, propane refrigerator, stove and lighting. There is a sleeping loft upstairs and two private bedrooms downstairs. We supply basic place settings for 8 and an assortment of pots, pans, and cookware to whip up some traditional Adirondack foods. On a clear day the view of Crane Mountain is superb.
