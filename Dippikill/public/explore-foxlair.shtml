Fox Lair is a rustic log cabin perched upon a hill snuggled away from the everyday. It holds up to four people. There is one bunk and a single couch bed. We supply five (5) gallons of water to start your stay. The cook stove provides both your heat and cooking needs. There is one table and place settings for four. The lodge is approximately 3/4 of a mile from the parking lot. 

FEATURES

- Rustic lodge about ¾ of a mile from the parking lot
- 4 person maximum occupancy
- 1 bunk bed, 1 couch bed and a folding cot
- Large cook stove for heat and meal times
- Cooking utensils and gear provided
- No lighting – we rent gas lanterns at the office for a small fee
- Refillable water container provided
- Campfire rings
- Stocked woodshed
