We offer a variety of tent sites to fit anyone's imagination.  Whether its a hike to the pond for a secluded retreat or a nice car camp adventure near the road we have you covered.  Take a look at both the numbered and lettered sites.  The numbered sites are at the pond and lettered sites are near the bathhouse.          

 

Camping sites have  restricted use from Oct. 1st to April 1st every year.
If you have any questions please contact the Dippikill Office.
