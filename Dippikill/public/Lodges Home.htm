Our lodging is based on basic principles:

 
Offer a clean lodge or campsite
 Keep the lodges unique.  We offer very primitive Adirondack style living to Farmhouse style luxuries to suite any needs.
There are different size lodges for different size groups.  The smaller lodges are great for a single person or couple and the larger ones offer  accommodations for student groups, reunions, and social events.   
All of our lodges are sited to give guests a comfort space.  Unlike many of the newer resorts there are acres separating our lodges.
We hope our guests can give themselves to the history and scenery of the Adirondacks.  Dippikill is  a place were the phones wont' ring, the TV doesn't' disturb a conversation a place were you can truly get away from the ordinary.    
The water at Dippikill is non-potable because it is not treated.  It is tested every 3 months.  We recommend you bring your own drinking water and boil water for 7 minutes for all domestic use.
