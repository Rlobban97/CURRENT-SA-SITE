From Albany:

Take the Northway (I-87) North towards Montreal to Exit 23
Turn onto Route 9 North and go through Warrensburg
Turn left onto Route 28 West and proceed another 5 miles to "The Glen"
After crossing the railroad tracks, turn left and go another 3 miles to a bridge
About 1/4 mile beyond the bridge turn left over another bridge
The office is 1/2 mile further up on the left  
Every vehicle must register at the office upon arrival

 

 

 

Alternate Winter/Inclemate Weather Directions From Albany:

Take the Northway (I-87) North towards Montreal to Exit 23
Turn onto Route 9 North and go through Warrensburg
Turn left onto Route 28 West and proceed another 5 miles, and go over the Hudson River.
After going over the Hudson River, turn left on to Glen-Athol Rd and go approximately 3.6 miles.
Take a right on to Parker Cross Rd, and another quick right onto Dippikill Rd.
Camp Dippikill is approximately 2.5 miles.  
If you come to Dippikill via this alternate route please make sure to sign in with the person on duty as they make their Friday evening rounds or in the morning at the office.
