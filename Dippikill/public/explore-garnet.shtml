Garnet Lodge is truly a favorite among many guests. Located on Dippikill Road, it is close to the bathhouse and White Pine Lodge. Garnet offers electricity, running water, refrigerator, stove, and place settings for up to eight (8) guests. The lodge is heated by a fireplace, which is constructed from locally mined "Garnet".
