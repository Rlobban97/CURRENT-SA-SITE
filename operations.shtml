Executive Office Manager

  Shannon Beal - Shannon Beal recently moved to the Capital area after nearly a decade working as an administrative support person, office manager, and artist in Queens, NY. She worked as an Assistant at Beech-Nut Nutrition and MTV, where she provided executive support and event planning for a team responsible for digital product innovation. She also served as studio manager and community liaison at The Space, a community arts organization in Long Island City. She graduated with a BFA from the Pratt Institute with a major in fine arts/painting. Shannon is looking forward to contributing to the Student Association of University at Albany, SUNY.
