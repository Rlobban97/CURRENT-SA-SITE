Chief of Staff

Langie Cadesca is a junior at the University at Albany, SUNY, who majors in Criminal Justice and has a minor in Africana Studies. At UAlbany, she is Chief of Staff to President Fontaine in the school’s governing body—the Student Association.

In the 2016-17 academic year, she served as Secretary of Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc., which is a community conscious, action-oriented organization. She was also the Public Relations Chair of Haitian Student Association (HSA), a community service organization that focuses on cultural initiatives. She is also a Residential Assistant on Indian Quad. She has interned with Senator Jesse Hamilton in the New York State Senate. During her freshman year, she was a Student Association Senator for Indian Quad and Ranking Member for the Constituent Relations Committee. She is also a member of EOP. She feels strongly that her involvement background has prepared her to serve the students by work alongside President Fontaine in the Student Association.

If you have any questions, you can reach Langie at lcadesca@albany.edu
