Department of Programming

Joey Fambrini is a senior at UAlbany majoring in Rhetoric and Communications and minoring in Business. Joey began his involvement with the Student Association during the fall semester of his sophomore year as a general intern before being promoted to assistant director of the programming department in the spring that same year.

Aside from working in the programming office, Joey loves to attend community service events and university events on campus. As programming director, Joey loves being a part of the department that plans events for the undergraduate student body and loves hearing student opinions and ideas. Joey also enjoys long walks on the podium and attending UAlbany sporting events.
